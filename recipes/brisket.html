Brisket

Description

Ingredients

  • Brisket
  • 2 Onions, cut up
  • 1 Green Pepper, cut up
  • 4 Cloves Garlic, chopped
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Squished Tomatoes
  • 1 tbs Sugar
  • 1 Boullion Cube
  • 1 packet Frenches Onion Gravy Mix

Steps

  1. Trim fat off brisket.
  2. Brown brisket on all sides.
  3. Spread mustard and ketchup on meat (separately)
  4. Put all in pot.
  5. Add squished tomatoes
  6. Add 1 tbs sugar
  7. Disolve boullion cube in 1 cup water and add.
  8. Cover and cook 2-3 hours.
  9. Check for softness of meat with fork.
  10. Remove meat.
  11. Strain juice into bowl.
  12. Add pack of onion gravy mix (Frenches) and some water.
  13. Put meat back in veg juices and gravy mix.
  14. Slice meat.
  15. Cover and warm in oven (optional)
